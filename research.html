Predictive Modeling to Assess Gentrification Susceptibility

I started researching gentrification susceptibility during my time in the Certificate in Geographic Information Systems program at The University of Washington-Tacoma. The results of that work can be viewed in the poster below.


I continued researching this topic during my Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies program and developed a tool that utilizes the Python 3.6 programming language to allow the user to quickly determine gentrification susceptibility anywhere in the United States.
